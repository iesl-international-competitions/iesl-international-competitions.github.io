How It Works

Start with your passion for science and engineering

Begin with your curiosity and innovative ideas in science and engineering. Let your passion fuel your journey towards discovery and making an impact.

Compete in national competitions

Showcase your projects in national competitions to refine your skills and ideas, laying the groundwork for further achievements.

Get shortlisted for international competition qualifiers

Stand out in national competitions to reach the qualifiers for international stages, challenging yourself against the best and broadening your horizons.

Win and represent Sri Lanka

Emerge victorious to represent Sri Lanka on prestigious international platforms like ISEF and RISE, becoming an ambassador for the country's young scientific talent.
